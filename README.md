# Oyster Bay Venture Capital

**Forging Future Food Champions.**

Oyster Bay is a European venture capital firm based in Hamburg, backing the next
generation of founders building in **food, beverage, and consumer goods
technology**. We invest early, we invest with conviction, and we stay close to
the teams we partner with.

> *No grit, no pearls.*

---

## What we back

We focus on companies shaping how Europe — and the world — produces, processes,
distributes, and consumes food and everyday consumer goods. Our thesis spans
the full value chain: from upstream ingredients and production technology to
brands, formats, and the infrastructure that moves products to the shelf.

We look for founders with deep sector conviction, a clear view of the
unit economics, and the patience to build durable businesses in a category
that rewards substance over hype.

## How we work with portfolio companies

- **Long-term capital partners**, not passive check-writers
- **Sector-literate sparring** across supply chain, brand, and go-to-market
- **Network access** to operators, acquirers, and co-investors across Europe
- **Honest dialogue** — we say what we see, and we expect the same in return

## This GitHub organisation

This org hosts the internal tooling our investment team uses to source,
evaluate, and track opportunities across the food and consumer-tech
ecosystem. Most repositories are private by design — they contain proprietary
research, pipelines, and data integrations that support our day-to-day
investment work.

If something here is public, it is intentionally so. If you are a founder,
co-investor, or service partner and need access to a specific repository,
please reach out through your usual contact at Oyster Bay.
